Ingredients
8 oz eggnog
4 tbsp ground coffee
whipped cream optional
cinnamon optional

Instructions
Place the ground coffee in the brew basket
Set mug in place to brew
Press the Specialty button
While coffee is brewing warm eggnog in the microwave for 30-45 seconds or warm on low heat on stovetop for 2-3 minutes
Froth eggnog for 30-45 seconds
Gently pour frothed eggnog into coffee
Top with whipped cream and cinnamon (optional)
